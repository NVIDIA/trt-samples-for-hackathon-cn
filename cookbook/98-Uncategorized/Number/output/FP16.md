# FP16 - IEEE754

+ Sign:     $s$ ($p = 1$ bit)
+ Exponent: $e$ ($q = 5$ bit)
+ Mantissa: $m$ ($r = 10$ bit)

+ Normal value ($00001_2 \le e_2 \le 11110_2$) (2 in subscript represents the base)

$$
\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
E &= e - \left( 2 ^ {q-1} - 1 \right) = e - 15 \\
M &= m \cdot 2 ^ {-r} = m \cdot 2 ^ {-10} \\
\text{value} &= \left( -1 \right) ^ {s} 2 ^ {E} \left( 1 + M \right) = \left( -1 \right) ^ {s} 2 ^ {-25} 2 ^ {e} \left( m + 2 ^ {10} \right)
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
$$

+ Subnormal value ($e_2 = 00000_2$)

$$
\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
E &= 2 - 2 ^ {q-1} = -14 \\
M &= m \cdot 2 ^ {-r} = m \cdot 2 ^ {-10} \\
\text{value} &= \left( -1 \right) ^ {s} 2 ^ {E} M = \left( -1 \right) ^ {s} 2 ^ {-24} m
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
$$

+ Special value

| Exponent | Mantissa | meaning |
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| all 0 | all 0 | Signed Zero |
| all 0 | not all 0 | Subnormal Value |
| all 1 | all 0 | Signed Infinity |
| all 1 | not all 0 | NaN |
